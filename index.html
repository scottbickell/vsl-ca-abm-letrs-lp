LETRS: Proven professional development for teachers of language and literacy

LETRS® (Language Essentials for Teachers of Reading and Spelling) provides educators with an in-depth understanding of the science behind teaching literacy and gives them the background and knowledge to teach language and literacy skills to every student.

The LETRS literacy PD solution is:

  • Flexible implementation—online, face to face, blended options
  • An evidence-based program aligned to Structured Literacy
  • Effective for teaching ALL students, including those with dyslexia
  • Relevant and applicable regardless of literacy programs already in use
